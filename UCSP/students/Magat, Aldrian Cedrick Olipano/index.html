What Is UCSP?

UCSP means understanding culture, society and politics.

It is the subject where we learn about our cultural backgrounds.

Things Need to Remember about UCSP

Politics Is the set of activities that are associated with making decisions in groups or other forms of power. The word politics comes from this Greek word Polis.
Society A group of individuals that will logether as one. From the root word Societas from latin.
Culture Is a way of life of an entire society from the latin word Cultura
