What is UCSP

Understanding Culture, Society, and Politics uses multidisciplinal insights from anthropology, political science, and sociology to develop student awareness of cultural, social, and political dynamics and sensitivity to cultural diversity.

ARTICLES

Culture

What is Culture

all the ways of life including arts, beliefs and institutions of a population that are passed down from generation to generation

Example:

Customs, laws, dress, architectural style, social standards, and traditions

ETHNOCENTRISM

What is Ethnocentrism

the attitude that one's own group, ethnicity, or nationality is superior to others

Example:

China took many islands that has many important sources and claimed it is their territory

a random latino man compares political system to filipino and thinks they had a better political system better

CULTURAL RELATIVISM

What is Cultural Relativism

not judging a culture to our own standards of what is right or wrong, strange or normal.

Example:

Anna uses her both hands handling something offered by her korean grandma to show some respect

kiss was not only romantic affection but also it is the sign of greeting in other countries

POLITICS

What is POLITICS

the activities associated with the governance of a country or other area, especially the debate or conflict among individuals or parties having or hoping to achieve power.

Example:

politicians making solutions in order to help it's people and fight against corruption and many problems the country faced with their power and influence

two politicans debating their plans and propagandas that convinces people to vote which one of them may be suit to become a new president of the country

RELIGION

What is RELIGION

the belief in and worship of a superhuman power or powers, especially a God or gods.

Example:

polytheism is belief of many gods while monotheism is belief of one god

muslims don't eat pork because their Lord "Allah" has prohibited the eating of pork and all unclean meat
