CULTURAL VARIATION

DEFINITION:

  • In terms of their beliefs, habits, practices, values, traditions, and other facets of their way of life, various cultures or cultural groups differ and are diverse from one another. This is referred to as cultural variance. These differences may appear in a variety of contexts, such as language, religion, social mores, cuisine, art, clothes, and others. Cultural diversity results from the various histories, circumstances, and experiences of various human communities.

    • EXAMPLES:

  • Language, religion, cuisine, clothing, social norms, art, family structures, customs, traditions, gender roles, and education systems significantly shape a society. Different cultures have unique beliefs, practices, food and cooking techniques, and social norms, resulting in diverse cultural identities.
