Trivia about G-IDLE

The meaning of the group's name: "(G)" (여자) is for "girl" and "Idle" (아이들) for "children".

The "I" in "I-DLE" is for individuality and "DLE" is plural, so the girls each have the individual personalities but they are related.

The name was given after Soyeon's single, "Idle Song".
